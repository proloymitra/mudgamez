Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026 | Effective Date: April 23, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. MUDGAMES is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

1. Who We Are

MUDGAMES Pvt Ltd ("MUDGAMES," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://mudgames.in (the "Website"), a free online gaming portal. Our registered office is at Technology Park, Whitefield, Bangalore, Karnataka, India - 560066.

Data Protection Officer: You can contact our DPO at dpo@mudgames.in for any privacy-related concerns.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with our Website:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Form Data: When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message content.
  • Game Submissions: If you submit a game, we collect developer information including name, email, company details, and game files.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Email address for sending game updates and promotional content (only with explicit consent).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • Usage Data: Games played, time spent on each game, click patterns, scroll behavior, and navigation paths.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, localStorage, and sessionStorage to enhance your experience (see Section 6 for details).
  • Theme Preference: Your light/dark theme choice is stored in localStorage and never transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Analytics 4: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to understand how players interact with our platform.
  • Google AdSense: Advertising data for serving personalized and non-personalized ads (see Section 7).
  • Social Media: If you interact with our social media pages, those platforms may share aggregated demographic data with us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Improve Services: To operate the gaming platform, load games correctly, and optimize performance based on device capabilities.
  • Personalization: To remember your theme preference (light/dark) and suggest games based on your play history.
  • Analytics: To understand which games are popular, identify technical issues, and improve user experience.
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdSense that support our free gaming model.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send newsletters (with consent), and notify you of important updates.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our platform.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For newsletter subscriptions, optional cookies, and personalized advertising. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests: For analytics, security, fraud prevention, and platform improvement.
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the gaming services you request.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Google LLC: Google Analytics 4 (usage analytics) and Google AdSense (advertising). Google's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: For storing website files and serving games via CDN.
  • Email Service Providers: For sending newsletters and responding to contact form submissions.

5.2 Game Developers

When you play a game hosted on our platform, the game developer may collect anonymized gameplay data directly through their game. Each developer operates under their own privacy policy. We require all developers to comply with applicable data protection laws.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better experience. Here's how we use them:

6.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your session state during gameplay.
  • localStorage: Store your theme preference (light/dark mode) and cookie consent choice.
  • CSRF Tokens: Protect form submissions from cross-site request forgery attacks.

6.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional — With Consent)

  • Google Analytics: _ga, _gid, _gat cookies for understanding website traffic and user behavior.

6.3 Advertising Cookies (Optional — With Consent)

  • Google AdSense: __gads, __gpi, NID cookies for serving relevant advertisements and measuring ad performance.

6.4 Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through:

  • Our Cookie Consent Banner (accept or reject non-essential cookies)
  • Your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge all offer cookie management)
  • Google Ad Settings to manage personalized ads
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out

7. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our platform. This helps us keep all 117 games free to play.

  • Personalized Ads: With your consent, Google may use your browsing history and interests to show relevant ads.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: If you reject cookies or opt out, you will still see ads, but they won't be based on your personal data.
  • Reward Ads: Some games offer optional reward ads (e.g., for bonus coins). These are entirely voluntary.
  • Ad Controls: You can control ad personalization at Google Ad Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to MUDGAMES and other sites. Learn more at Google's Ad Technologies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Contact Form Submissions: Retained for 2 years for customer service purposes, then deleted.
  • Analytics Data: Google Analytics retains data for 14 months (configurable in Google settings).
  • Advertising Data: Google AdSense retains data per their own retention policies.
  • Theme Preference: Stored in localStorage indefinitely until you clear your browser data.

9. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

9.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Residents)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

9.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information collected from you.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data, but you may opt-out of personalized advertising).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

9.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at dpo@mudgames.in with the subject line "Privacy Rights Request." We will respond within 30 days. For verification, we may ask for proof of identity.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission (TLS 1.3)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems
  • Data minimization — we collect only what is necessary
  • Regular staff training on data protection practices

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our platform is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at dpo@mudgames.in and we will promptly delete such information.

For children aged 13-16, we recommend parental supervision while using the platform. Some games may contain mild cartoon violence; parental guidance is advised.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required by GDPR.
